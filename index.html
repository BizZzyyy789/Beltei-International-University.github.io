About us

            History

BELTEI International University is a private higher education institution 2002,

developed from BELTEI International Institute which was founded on January 01,

by the Prakas of Ministry of Education.BELTEI is the acronym consisting of 6main
subject BEL comes from Business, Economics, Law and TEI come from Tourism,
                                         English, Information Technology.

FOUNDER

H.E. Dr. LY Chheng

H.E. Dr. LY Chheng, is a former of BELTEI International Institute and an Advisor to the Council of Ministers and at the present, a member of parliament for Phnom Penh Capital, the Director-General of BELTEI Group and the President of BELTEI International University as well as founder of BELTEI by investing his own private funding to build all these BELTEI School and university campuses.
